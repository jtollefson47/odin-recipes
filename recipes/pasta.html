Pasta Recipe


Pasta icons created by Freepik - Flaticon

This is a simple and delicious pasta recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted.
  6. Drain the pasta and toss with the sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
