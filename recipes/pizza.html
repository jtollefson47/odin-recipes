Pizza Recipe


Pizza icons created by Freepik - Flaticon

This is a simple and delicious pizza recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni, sliced
  • 1/2 cup green peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup onions, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni, green peppers, and onions on top.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
